The rules

    Hands:
    This rule only goes to the players that are not in goal because they are not allowed to hold the ball in there hands or it will be cheating and the other team will get a freekick, or a penalty if they touched it inside the box.
    Corner Kicks & Goal Kicks
    Corner kicks and goal kicks only happen when the other team shoots the ball out of the goal line. if the opposing team shoots the ball there it is a goal kick, if the other team shoots there it is a corner kick.
    Fouls
    Fouls are when a player injures another player. If a player gets injured the player that injured him will either get a red card, a yellow card or a warning.
    Direct and Indirect Free Kicks
    Direct freekicks is when the player shoots the ball directly to the goal and does not pass. Indirect freekicks is when the player passes to his team first.
    Penalty Kicks
    Penalty kicks are when a player injures or touches the ball with his hand in his own teams box. If he does so there will be a penalty kick. If there is a penalty the player in the opposing team will put the ball on the white dot inide the box andhe will shoot.
    Off side
    Off side is when a player attacks and there is less than two opposition players against him. If this happens the opposing team will get a free kick.
